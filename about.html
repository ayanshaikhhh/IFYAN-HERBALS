Who We Are

Established in 2017 in Sojat, Rajasthan – the heartland of henna production – Marwadi Mehandi is a trusted manufacturer, exporter, wholesaler, and supplier of premium quality henna powder. We are proud to be listed among the leading names in the industry, delivering a wide range of natural henna and hair care products to meet diverse customer needs.

Our Product Range

We specialize in offering 100% pure and chemical-free products, including:

Henna Cone, Multani Mitti, Face Packs, Henna Powder, Marwadi Mehandi Color, Amla Powder, Areetha (Soapnut) Powder, Shikakai Powder, Aloe Vera Powder, Rose Peel Powder, Brahmi Powder, Bhringraj Powder, Indigo Powder, Other Natural Herbs.

Commitment to Quality

At Marwadi Mehandi, we prioritize quality above all. Our skilled and dedicated team ensures that each product undergoes rigorous quality checks to guarantee purity and authenticity. We strive to deliver 100% natural products, free from harmful chemicals, ensuring the best results for our customers.
